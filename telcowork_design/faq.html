Q : How do I register into Telcowork platform ?

Q : I am a Telco professional working full time, but I have programming skills can I search and work as freelancer using Telcowork platform?

A : Yes you can, as long you can manage your time well, your service can be useful for someone who looking for tools maker, such as database, data processing, or report generator tools/automation. In Telco projects, we often use different tools on each project, as the project and customers are unique almost all the time.

A : You can get tools makers, drive tester and even higher level RF consultant through our website, our database of professional talents are consist of many Indonesian talents, their resume is trusted and cross checked by our team, ensuring their skills are as they have said on resume.

A : By join us, you will get access to multiple projects all around Indonesia, you can select which suit you best in term of work location, customers, compensation value, and project scope.
