Who We Are

Dynamic is a nature, most of us never expect a downfall of telecommunication industry. Like every other industry that all stakeholder demand a growth. When market is saturated and competition getting taft, one of last trick is to carry out an efficiency to maintain its business growth

That last trick was stricking out all parties in telecommunication industry not only telecommunication talent which suffer from lower wage due to lower job value and shorter contract due to project circumtances but also come up to telecom company which getting less margin either due to lower project value with more task or more idle time of worker due to project circumtances.

Another things that vastly changing in telecommunication industry is technology, almost less than a decade new technology was introduce in this industry that required huge investment for telecom provider but also required talent with vastly upgrade of knowledge.

That latter point is doubling the burden to both telecom company and talent. Last but not least, digital era are coming up in our age and free trade zone became widely implemented in most country including south east asia. Competition are getting more harsh.

We, as part of Indonesian telecom part, are motivated to create a creative website platform as an answer to this cirmuntances. Offering more “value” to both talent and telecom company by cutting off chain, providing more opportunity for talents to get most suitable job, location and prive to thems while offering more flexibility, less idle time and eventually cutting off over head cost.

Telcowork platform also offering one stop digital platform on telecommunication by engage training company which offer almost all training to keep talent knowledge update and supporting company for any rent vehicle or supporting tools required to complete the job opportunity. Digital library and telecom forum are design to provide online knowledge library and a home for us to discuss many things. Nothing is stronger than togetherness

So enjoy, together we can !!!!
